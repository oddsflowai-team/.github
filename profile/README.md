# OddsFlow AI (oddsflow.ai)

Evidence-first football market analytics with public, timestamped verification logs.  
No hype. Just logs.

## What we publish
- Pre-match Signal Audits (evidence-first checklist)
- Live match signals explained (intent / threat / shot quality)
- Post-match verification (timestamps + performance logs)
- Research notes on probability, pricing, and market mechanics
